A **DDoS attack** is an attempt to make a service unavailable by overwhelming a [[Network]], server, or application with a large volume of traffic originating from multiple compromised [Computer](obsidian://open?vault=Obsidian&file=Tech%2FHardware%2FConcepts%2FComputer) or devices. This flood of traffic clogs resources, creating a bottleneck that disrupts or disables the availability of the targeted service
