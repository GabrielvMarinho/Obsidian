1. What are three types of architecture personalities? What type of boundary does
each personality create?
- the control freak: creates too much control and dont let the team picks its own implementations and tools
- the armchair architect: let the team work freely, choosing between implementation and tools
- the efficient architect: understand that those approaches form a spectrum and that it should be somewhere between that depending on the teams experience, time to develop, etc.

2. What are the five factors that go into determining the level of control you should
exhibit on the team?
- experience of the team
- time to develop
- complexity
- team familiarity
- team size

3. What are three warning signs you can look at to determine if your team is getting
too big?
- process loss
- pluralistic ignorance
- diffusion of responsibility

4. List three basic checklists that would be good for a development team.
- configuration changes in servers 
- third party libraries added
- database updates and migrations
