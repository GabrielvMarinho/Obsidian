To conjugate a regelmassige verben you have to cut the **en** and work with the stem:

### Present

**Mach** - en 

And conjugate with the Person singular and person plural:

**Ich** - e
**du** - st
**er / sie / es** - t 
**wir** - en
**ihr** - t
**sie / Sie** -  en

Examples:

Was mach**st** du gerade?
What are you doing right now?

ich schaue youtube.
im watching youtube.
